Thought of as the essence of being by many cultures, breath and proper breathing technique is one of the most beneficial things that can be done for both short and long term physical and emotional health. Deep breathing or Abdominal breathing technique when performed regularly alleviates stress and leads to increased cardio-vascular health. This app gives you a visual cue on the correct breathing technique.

Steps:

  • Start by taking a deep breath from the lower part of your chest.
  • Notice how the color of body frame in the app fills up from bottom up, regulate your rhythm to match the animation.
  • Slowly exhale on the same rhythm as the color is emptied in the body frame.
  • Repeat the cycle of breaths for as long as you are comfortable by following the rhythm in the application.
  • Take care to avoid any noise, shallowness, jerkiness or pauses during inhalation and exhalation.

Once comfortable with the above technique, you may want to incorporate positive words or those familiar with yoga can use any mantra to enhance your experience.
